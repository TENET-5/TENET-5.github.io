How to Read The Board

Every node on this board represents a documented public record: a government contract, a lobbying registration, a campaign donation, a corporate directorship, or an ethics violation. The red threads connect entities that share documented relationships — money flows, corporate links, lobbying relationships, or ethics findings.

Sources: BuyAndSell.gc.ca (federal procurement), Elections Canada (campaign finance), Office of the Commissioner of Lobbying (lobbyist registry), provincial corporate registries, Ethics Commissioner rulings, Auditor General reports, Charbonneau Commission evidence, and court records.

This is not speculation. Every connection is sourced from public record.
