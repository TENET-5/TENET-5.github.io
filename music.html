Why This Music Matters

Dead Kennedys understood something that most people learn too late: institutions protect themselves, not the people they claim to serve. When Jello Biafra was prosecuted for "distributing harmful matter" over album artwork, it was the same playbook — use the legal system as a weapon to silence dissent.

The parallels to what's happening in Canada right now are hard to ignore. When a veteran reports foreign interference and gets prosecuted instead of protected, the system is working exactly as designed — just not for us.

This music is the soundtrack to that fight. Turn it up.

"The government has turned the justice system into a weapon against the people it's supposed to protect. That's not a conspiracy theory. That's Tuesday." — Daniel Perry, PPCLI Veteran
