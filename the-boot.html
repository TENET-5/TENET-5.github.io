02 — THE FORMULABoot Size Determination

The size of the corrective boot is proportional to the magnitude of the failure, weighted by the human cost and adjusted for institutional indifference.

The Boot Equation

BootSize = W × D / (P × Y) + T / B × ln(R) = ($200B × 130,000) / (40M × 141) + ($7.5B / $8M) × ln(738) = 4,609,929 + 937.5 × 6.604 = 4,609,929 + 6,191 = 4,616,120 Boot Units (BU)

Where 1 BU = 1 standard Canadian combat boot (size 10, issued to Canadian Forces infantry).

05 — THE INDIFFERENCE COEFFICIENTWhy the Boot Must Be Large

The Boot Size is not arbitrary. It is directly proportional to the Indifference Coefficient — a measure of how many warnings were ignored before consequences occurred.

Indifference Coefficient (IC)

IC = (Warnings Given × Reports Written) / Ministers Jailed = (738 records × 47 AG reports) / 0 = UNDEFINED (division by zero) The Indifference Coefficient is mathematically undefined because no federal minister has ever served prison time. Division by zero is appropriate — it represents infinite indifference.

When the Indifference Coefficient approaches infinity, the Boot Size also approaches infinity. This is not a flaw in the mathematics — it is a flaw in the government.

06 — THE PHOENIX CORRECTIONAdjusting for IT Incompetence

The government has spent $7.5 billion on a pay system that doesn’t work. To calculate the IT Incompetence Factor:

IT Incompetence Factor

ITF = Phoenix Cost / Cost of Shopify = $7,500,000,000 / $29.99/month = 250,083,361 months of Shopify = 20,840,280 years For the cost of Phoenix, the government could have run a Shopify subscription for 20.8 million years. Shopify actually processes payments.

The Verdict

4,616,120 BU

The mathematically determined boot size required to correct 141 years of documented government failure is 4,616,120 standard Canadian combat boots — stacked 1,385 km high, weighing 4,616 tonnes, delivering 45.3 meganewtons of corrective force.

The Indifference Coefficient remains undefined because no minister has ever gone to prison.
